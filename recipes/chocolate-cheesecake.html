Chocolate Cheesecake

Description

In this no-bake double chocolate cheesecake, a chocolate cookie crust comes together with a chocolate-cocoa filling for an epic oven-free dessert.

Ingredients

  • 13 creme-filled chocolate sandwich cookies, crushed, plus more for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 10 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder, sifted
  • 1/4 cup hot water
  • (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 cups heavy cream
